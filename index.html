IIITNR CURRICULUM

The iiitnr-curriculum incorporates current developments in academic areas as well as course structuring innovations such as 'Fractal Academics' and 'Skill Orientation.' The unusual notion of 'Fractal Academics,' which originated at Carnegie Melon University in the United States, is now being implemented in some of India's new IITs.

Find Out More about iiitnr-curriculum

Academics

The three undergraduate programmes provided by iiitnr-curriculum are B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and B.Tech. in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI). These credit-based programmes in our curriculum are designed to help students develop important skills like creativity and entrepreneurship. The dynamic structure of the programme responds to the changing demands of industry and academia. The Fractal Curriculum is followed at IIIT Naya Raipur.
